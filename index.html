About the Lab

The Seaborne Lab investigates how individual skeletal muscle fibers behave and adapt. Using cutting-edge Muscle-Omics technologies, we study molecular regulation, myofiber heterogeneity, and the impact of aging and neuromuscular disease.

Our lab focuses on understanding the molecular programmes that control myofiber function and how these processes are disrupted in disease.
